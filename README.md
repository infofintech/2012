# POPCRUSH PROJECT 2016
A free web collection of music from around the world.

_"I can understand wanting to have millions of dollars, there’s a certain freedom, meaningful freedom, that comes with that. But once you get much beyond that, I have to tell you, it’s the same hamburger." - Bill Gates_

## Composition
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2015 until the end of 2017 (median year 2016). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned or unheard of: Ariana Grande, T-PAIN (previously unmentioned in Pitbull - Hey Baby song), Taylor Swift, Alan Walker, Drake, DNCE, Eric Saade, Calvin Harris (previously unmentioned in Rihanna - We Found Love song), Chainsmokers, Kanye West, Katharine McPhee, Zara Larsson, Zendaya, etc.
