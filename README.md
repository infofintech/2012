# Popcrush Project 2016
A free web collection of music from around the world.

_"I can understand wanting to have millions of dollars, there’s a certain freedom, meaningful freedom, that comes with that. But once you get much beyond that, I have to tell you, it’s the same hamburger." - Bill Gates_

## Composition
This collection is a blend of two music collections from the different time periods.

### Music from the period 2013-2015
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2013 until the end of 2015 (median year 2014). The artists of these songs are including: Shakira, Rihanna, Chris Brown, Dony, Elena Gheorghe, INNA, Otilia, Bruno Mars, Jessie J, Will.i.am, Mamasita, Pitbull, PH Electro, Britney Spears, Becky G, and more.

### Music from the period 2015-2017
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2015 until the end of 2017 (median year 2016). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned[^1], factually unmentioned[^2] or unheard of[^3]: Ariana Grande, T-PAIN (previously unmentioned in Pitbull - Hey Baby song), Taylor Swift, Alan Walker, Drake, DNCE, Eric Saade, Calvin Harris (previously unmentioned in Rihanna - We Found Love song), Chainsmokers, Katharine McPhee, Zara Larsson, Zendaya, etc.
[^1]: Included in the song from the previous package of same domain, but not mentioned.
[^2]: Included in the song from the package of another content domain, but not mentioned.
[^3]: Leading singer that was NEWLY added, missing in previous package of same domain.
